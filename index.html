About  Us

Who We Are

We are changing the way how people use salon services by making the whole experience super convenient and flexible - for both users and salon owners. 'Beautiva' gives you the freedom to chose what you want, when you want and where you want when it comes to grooming yourself. Everything of your choice, at your convenience!

A uniquely designed Website crafted specially for Salon services, brings users and salons together giving them the variety of options - for users to explore and access better; and for salon owners to manage and cater to services more efficiently.

Our Awards

Our stylists earned the prestigious "Best of Boston"® award in 2022 and 2023, recognized by Boston magazine. They were also honored by ELLE magazine as one of the Top 100 Salons in the US for both years. These accolades highlight our team's talent and dedication to providing exceptional salon experiences and showcase our commitment to excellence in the beauty industry.
